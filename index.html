What You Receive

Everything covered. Nothing out of pocket.

GAPP is a Medicaid benefit fully state-funded that your child is entitled to if they qualify.

01

In-Home Skilled Nursing

Licensed RNs and LPNs provide medical-level care in your home trach care, tube feeding, medications, vitals with expertise and compassion.

02

Personal Care Support

Bathing, dressing, hygiene, repositioning, and mobility support that helps your child thrive and reduces the physical toll on you.

03

Parents Can Be Paid

Qualifying parents may be compensated as official caregivers so the best care stays personal and income stays in the family.

04

No Cost to the Family

No co-pays, no deductibles, no invoices. Medicaid covers 100% of approved GAPP services for all qualified families.

05

No Waitlist

Unlike many state programs, GAPP has no waitlist. Once approved, care can begin quickly often within days of authorization.

06

Custom Care Plan

You help shape every detail. Every service is tailored to your child's specific medical needs and personal goals.

Services Included

What your child will receive

All services performed by licensed professionals in the comfort of your home.

Skilled Nursing Services

Provided by licensed RNs & LPNs
  • Medication administration & management
  • Tracheostomy care, cleaning & tube changes
  • Gastrostomy tube feeding, care & changing
  • Respiratory treatments & suctioning
  • Vital signs monitoring & documentation
  • Wound care & dressing changes
  • Bagging via trach or mouth
  • Reporting & coordination with care physicians

Personal Care Services

Daily living support & assistance
  • Bathing, grooming & personal hygiene
  • Dressing & appearance assistance
  • Turning, repositioning & skin care
  • Wheelchair mobility & transfers
  • Feeding assistance & nutrition support
  • Linen changes & room care
  • Daily living activity assistance
  • Observation, monitoring & wellness support
